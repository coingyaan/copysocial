About CopySocial

Making social media content extraction simple, free, and accessible for everyone

Our Mission

CopySocial was built to solve a simple but frustrating problem: extracting text from social media posts should be easy and free.

Content creators, recruiters, marketers, and professionals waste hours manually copying text from LinkedIn posts and Twitter threads. We built CopySocial to give that time back to you.

Our Goal: Provide the fastest, most reliable, and completely free tool to extract text from LinkedIn posts and Twitter/X threads - no login required, no hidden fees, no limitations.

Why CopySocial Exists

The idea for CopySocial came from real-world frustration. As a content creator and recruiter, I constantly needed to:

• Copy text from LinkedIn posts to repurpose on other platforms
• Extract Twitter threads to save as references
• Build content portfolios from social media posts
• Save inspiring posts for later without screenshots

Every existing solution was either paid, required login, had daily limits, or was unreliable. So I built CopySocial - a tool I wish existed when I needed it.

What Makes Us Different

100% Free Forever: No freemium model, no premium plans, no hidden costs. CopySocial is and will always be completely free.

No Login Required: We don't ask for your LinkedIn or Twitter credentials. We don't track you. We don't store your data. Just paste the URL and extract.

Built for Real Use Cases: Whether you're a content creator repurposing posts, a recruiter building portfolios, or a marketer analyzing competitors, CopySocial works for you.

Privacy First: We don't store extracted content. We don't log your activity. Your content extraction happens in real-time and nothing is saved.

Built by Real Users, For Real Users
