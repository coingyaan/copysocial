About CopySocial

CopySocial is a free social media utility designed to help creators, recruiters, researchers, and marketers quickly copy text from public social media posts.

Many social media platforms make it difficult to copy text directly from posts, especially on mobile devices. CopySocial solves that problem by allowing users to paste a post URL and instantly extract the text.

What CopySocial Supports

  • LinkedIn Post Copier
  • Twitter / X Thread Copier (coming soon)
  • Instagram Caption Copier (coming soon)

CopySocial is built to simplify social media research and content analysis while respecting platform guidelines and public content access.
