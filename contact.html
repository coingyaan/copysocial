Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?
Email us at hello@coingyaan.com with "BUG REPORT" in the subject line. Please include: the URL you tried to extract, what went wrong, and any error messages you saw. Screenshots are helpful!
Can I suggest a new feature?
Absolutely! We love hearing feature ideas from users. Email us with "FEATURE REQUEST" in the subject and describe what you'd like to see. We review all suggestions and prioritize based on user demand.
Why isn't my extraction working?
First, make sure the post is public (not private or restricted). If it's public and still not working, the post might be deleted or the platform changed their structure. Email us with the URL and we'll investigate.
Do you offer priority support?
CopySocial is 100% free and we provide support to all users equally. We respond to emails in the order received, typically within 24 hours.
Can I collaborate with CopySocial?
Yes! We're open to partnerships, integrations, and collaborations. Email us with "PARTNERSHIP" in the subject line and tell us about your idea.
How can I support CopySocial?
The best way to support us is to share CopySocial with others who might find it useful! Spread the word on LinkedIn, Twitter, or your blog. Every share helps us reach more people.

Before You Email...

To help us respond faster, please include:

For Bug Reports:
• The exact URL you tried to extract
• What browser you're using (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
• What happened vs what you expected
• Screenshots if possible

For Feature Requests:
• What problem are you trying to solve?
• How would this feature help you?
• Any examples from other tools?

For General Questions:
• Check our blog first - we have guides and FAQs
• Be as specific as possible about your question
• Let us know if it's time-sensitive
