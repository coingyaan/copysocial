Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or sell your data. We use Google Analytics for basic traffic stats. That's it.

Introduction

CopySocial ("we", "our", or "us") operates copysocial.co (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service to extract text from LinkedIn posts and Twitter/X threads.

We believe in transparency and privacy. This policy is written in plain English because legal jargon shouldn't hide what we do with your data (which is almost nothing).

What Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Zero. We don't require accounts, logins, or any personal information to use CopySocial. You don't give us your name, email, phone number, or anything else.

URLs You Extract

When you paste a LinkedIn or Twitter URL to extract text, we process that URL in real-time through our servers. We do not store these URLs. The extraction happens, you get the text, and we forget the URL immediately.

Extracted Content

The text content you extract from social media posts is displayed to you in your browser. We do not store this content. It exists only in your browser session and disappears when you close the page.

Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use CopySocial. This collects:

  • Pages you visit on copysocial.co
  • How long you spend on the site
  • What country/city you're accessing from (approximate location)
  • Your browser type (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

What We DON'T Collect

Let's be crystal clear about what we DON'T do:

  • Your Social Media Credentials: We never ask for or store your LinkedIn or Twitter login information
  • Your Email Address: No account required means no email collection
  • Extracted URLs: We don't keep a database of what URLs you've extracted
  • Extracted Content: The text you extract is not stored on our servers
  • Your Search History: We don't track what you're searching for or extracting
  • IP Addresses: We don't log or store IP addresses (Google Analytics may collect approximate location)
  • Personally Identifiable Information: Nothing that can identify you as an individual

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect through Google Analytics is used only to:

  • Understand how many people use CopySocial
  • See which features are most popular
  • Identify technical issues or bugs
  • Improve the user experience

We do NOT use your information to:

  • Serve you personalized ads
  • Sell to third parties
  • Build user profiles
  • Track you across other websites
  • Send you marketing emails (we don't have your email anyway)

Cookies

CopySocial uses minimal cookies:

  • Google Analytics Cookies: Track site usage statistics (you can opt out)
  • No Third-Party Advertising Cookies: We don't serve ads, so no ad cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but Google Analytics may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

For website traffic analysis. Read Google's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare

For website hosting and DDoS protection. Read Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Social Media Platforms

When you extract content, our servers make requests to LinkedIn and Twitter to fetch publicly available content. We don't authenticate with these platforms or access private data.

Data Security

Since we don't store your data, there's minimal security risk. However:

  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • We don't store passwords because we don't have accounts
  • We don't store extracted content or URLs
  • Our infrastructure is hosted on Cloudflare's secure servers

Your Rights

Under privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA, you have rights regarding your data. Since we don't collect or store personal information, most of these rights don't apply. But here's what you can do:

  • Right to Know: This Privacy Policy explains what we collect (almost nothing)
  • Right to Delete: Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to delete
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Right to Access: You can request any data we have about you (spoiler: we have none)

To exercise any rights or ask questions, email us at hello@coingyaan.com.

Children's Privacy

CopySocial is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us.

International Users

CopySocial is operated from India but accessible worldwide. If you're accessing from the EU, California, or other regions with specific privacy laws, you have the rights mentioned above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of CopySocial after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us
